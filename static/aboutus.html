Our history

The history of this service dates back to when a group of four volunteers: Yulia, Jiya, Olena and Polina realised that they could work separately anymore. They needed a service to put their work together, but didn't have one. That's when the idea to create one appeared. Founded in response to Ukraine's need for strong and organized support, our volunteer company has been actively providing aid to the military and frontline defenders since its inception.

Our aims and plans

Our aim is to mobilize resources, coordinate logistics, and deliver essential supplies, ensuring soldiers have the necessary equipment, medical aid, and humanitarian support. Looking ahead, we plan to expand our network, strengthen international partnerships, and develop sustainable aid programs to provide long-term assistance in Ukraine’s fight for freedom and security.

Our team

Olenka

Olenka is a passionate volunteer who thrives in organizing events and bringing people together. With a warm heart and sharp mind, she ensures every detail is handled. Her energy uplifts the team, and she’s always the first to offer help — whether it’s logistics or just a comforting word.

Yulia

Yulia is a hands-on problem-solver with a love for action. You’ll find her on the ground, making sure supplies reach the right people or leading youth workshops with contagious enthusiasm and unwavering focus. She’s not afraid to get her hands dirty — her impact is as real and lasting as it gets.

Polina

Polina handles internal communication and logistics. She creates volunteer shifts, writes internal guides, and oversees supply inventory. Thanks to her, everyone knows where to be, what to bring, and who to ask. She’s the quiet engine that makes big things happen without chaos.

Jiya

Jiya is the heartbeat of any volunteer group — dependable, driven, and deeply caring. She’s the one making checklists, coordinating schedules, and still finding time to bring snacks for everyone. With her strong sense of responsibility and gentle humor, she keeps everything — and everyone — running smoothly.
